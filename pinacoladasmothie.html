PIÑA COLADA SMOOTHIE

    INGREDIENTS

    SERVES 1, MAKES 500 ML

  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • ½ medium banana
  • ¼ cup ice cubes
  • 2 dates, seedless
  • ⅛ teaspoon vanilla bean powder (optional)

    • METHOD

    Place everything into a blender and blend until smooth. Serve.
